Project Overview

This project focuses on the accurate 3D reconstruction of my college building, modeled in Blender with close attention to real-world dimensions, proportions, and architectural details. The objective was to replicate the structure as precisely as possible, maintaining the exact scale, layout, and spatial relationships found in the actual campus building. From the primary structural framework to individual elements such as walls, floors, windows, and stairways, every component was created based on careful observation and measurement to achieve a faithful digital representation.

A strong emphasis was placed on realism through detailed texturing, material accuracy, and lighting setups that reflect real environmental conditions. Project Key Features

Accurate Architectural Reconstruction

Recreated the college building in Blender with real-world scale and proportions, focusing on structural accuracy, floor layouts, wall thickness, and architectural alignment based on the original building design.

Realistic Materials & Texturing

Applied accurate material properties to match real surfaces such as concrete, glass, painted walls, and flooring, ensuring visual realism and consistency across the entire model.

Physically Based Lighting

Designed lighting setups to simulate natural daylight and ambient conditions, enhancing depth, shadows, and realism while maintaining accurate architectural visibility.

Campus Context & Surroundings

Incorporated surrounding elements such as pathways, greenery, and open spaces to reflect the real campus environment and provide spatial context to the building model.
