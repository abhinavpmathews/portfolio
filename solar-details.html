Project Overview

This project presents a virtual recreation of the solar system developed using Unity 3D, designed to accurately represent the spatial arrangement, scale relationships, and motion of celestial bodies. Each planet was modeled and positioned to reflect its relative size and orbital path, creating an immersive and educational 3D experience that allows users to explore the solar system in an interactive environment.

A strong emphasis was placed on visual realism through physically based materials, high-quality textures, and carefully tuned lighting setups that simulate natural space illumination. Realistic light falloff, shadows, and reflections were used to enhance depth and scale, resulting in a visually compelling and scientifically inspired simulation. This project demonstrates proficiency in Unity 3D, real-time rendering, and the creation of accurate, visually rich virtual environments.

Project Key Features

Accurate Planetary Representation

Modeled and positioned planets to reflect realistic size ratios, orbital paths, and spatial relationships, creating an authentic representation of the solar system within a 3D environment.

High-Quality Materials & Textures

Applied detailed planetary textures and physically based materials to accurately represent surface variations, atmospheres, and visual characteristics of each celestial body.

Realistic Space Lighting

Implemented precise lighting setups to simulate solar illumination, realistic light falloff, shadows, and reflections, enhancing depth and realism across the entire solar system.

Interactive Exploration Environment

Designed an immersive space environment allowing users to freely explore planets, observe orbital motion, and experience the scale and structure of the solar system interactively.
