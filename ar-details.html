Project Overview

This project showcases an Augmented Reality (AR) application developed using Unity 3D, where scanning an image of a car triggers the display of a detailed 3D AR model of that vehicle. Image recognition is used to accurately detect and track the target image, allowing the virtual car to appear anchored in the real world with correct scale, orientation, and positioning for an immersive AR experience.

A strong emphasis was placed on realism and user interaction by applying high-quality materials, accurate textures, and realistic lighting adapted for augmented environments. The AR model responds smoothly to camera movement, maintaining visual stability and depth perception. This project demonstrates practical knowledge of augmented reality workflows, image tracking, and real-time rendering using Unity 3D, highlighting the ability to create interactive and visually convincing AR applications.

Project Key Features

Image-Based AR Tracking

Implemented image recognition to detect a target car image and accurately anchor a 3D car model in the real world, ensuring correct positioning, scale, and orientation within the AR environment.

Realistic 3D Car Visualization

Used high-quality car models with detailed materials and textures to accurately represent real vehicle surfaces, enhancing realism and visual clarity in augmented reality.

AR-Optimized Lighting

Adjusted lighting and shading to blend virtual objects naturally with real-world surroundings, maintaining consistent illumination, shadows, and depth perception in the AR scene.

Interactive AR Experience

Enabled smooth user interaction by allowing real-time viewing from different angles through camera movement, providing an immersive and engaging augmented reality experience.
